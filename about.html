Community project

Built by Frame VII, for Frame VII editors.

MLCLIPS exists so editors stop wasting time screen-recording their own games and start pulling clean, tagged footage straight from the source.

Who it's for

Video editors, content creators, and montage builders who need raw, high quality Mobile Legends footage without recording it themselves.

How it works

Owners upload clips with their Google Drive file ID and a few details like hero and skin. The site auto-generates the thumbnail, preview, and download link — no manual linking needed.

Stay credited

Every clip card shows the original owner, so editors can credit footage and the community keeps growing.
